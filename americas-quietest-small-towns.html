Methodology

How we built the most accurate quiet-town ranking available.

Data Source

  • U.S. DOT Bureau of Transportation Statistics
  • National Transportation Noise Map (2020)
  • Combined aviation, highway, and rail noise
  • Verified with visual map inspection

Scoring Formula

  • Base score from population + distance metrics
  • Adjusted by actual measured dB levels
  • +5 to +10 bonus for exceptionally quiet (<45 dB)
  • -10 to -50 penalty for noise (>50 dB)

What We Measured

  • Distance to nearest commercial airport
  • Distance to nearest interstate highway
  • Population density
  • Environmental type (desert, mountain, coastal, forest)

Map Verification

  • Every town checked against DOT noise map
  • Formula scores adjusted for ground truth
  • Hidden noise sources identified (rail, flight paths)
  • Acoustic traps noted (canyons, valleys)
